# Tone And Risk Pass

Use this workflow to make a page more neutral, factual, accessible, and legally safer.

## Skills

- `crw-style-lint`
- `crw-source-auditor`
- `crw-ai-policy-finalizer`
- `crw-edit-packager`

## Steps

1. Fetch or paste the page.
2. Identify risky tone and unsupported conclusions.
3. Replace accusations with sourced attribution.
4. Simplify jargon and overtechnical details.
5. Preserve valid factual content.
6. Verify citations still support the revised text.
7. Package the edit.

## Output

- Risk findings.
- Suggested wording changes.
- Source notes.
- Suggested edit summary.
