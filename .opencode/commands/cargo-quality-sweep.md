Use `workflows/cargo-quality-sweep.md`.

Load and follow these skills:

- `crw-cargo-triage`
- `crw-cargo-research-improver`
- `crw-cargo-infobox-fixer`
- `crw-relationship-mapper`
- `crw-source-auditor`
- `crw-style-lint`
- `crw-ai-policy-finalizer`
- `crw-edit-packager`

Start from Cargo quality findings as a discovery queue, not as the whole task. Pick a focused set of affected pages, inspect current wiki content, research each subject using reliable sources, and produce a report with article improvements, Cargo fixes, source notes, and suggested patches.

Do not stop at "missing field" advice. Use research to improve the article where useful: lead text, factual accuracy, citations, relationships, concise descriptions, incident status/date fields, and parent/incident separation. Do not publish wiki edits.
