# CRW Title And Link Normalizer

Use this skill when reviewing page names, headings, internal links, or external context links.

## Title And Heading Rules

- Use sentence case.
- Use concise nouns or noun phrases.
- Do not begin with "A", "An", or "The" unless part of a proper name.
- Avoid punctuation unless part of a name.
- Do not end titles or headings with periods.
- Use only necessary special characters, normally dashes, parentheses, and ampersands.
- Create redirects for hyphenated/unhyphenated equivalents when appropriate.

## Link Rules

- Link the first meaningful mention of CRW entities to internal CRW pages where available.
- Use Wikipedia links only for extra context where an internal CRW page cannot provide it.
- Do not link stub Wikipedia pages for context.
- Link iFixit at first mention of a specific electrical product model when a useful iFixit page exists.
- Put official company/product/institution websites in infoboxes when possible.
- Do not use page links as evidence for factual claims.

## Output

Return:

- Title/heading findings.
- Link findings.
- Redirect recommendations.
- Suggested minimal edits.
